Controls

Up/Down arrows: Menu navigation
In game: Up increases Y, Down decreases Y
Left/Right arrows: change X
Enter: Interact/Confirm
B: Speak current zone
C: Speak coordinates
G: Speak gold
T: Trade at merchant zone
V: Save to slot menu
Esc: Close open panel / return menu
